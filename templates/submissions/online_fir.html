What is an e-FIR?

An e-FIR is a First Information Report filed electronically through an online portal. Many Indian states now offer this service for certain types of complaints, allowing citizens to report incidents without visiting a police station in person.

Important

  • These are external links to official government websites.
  • The facility to file an e-FIR may vary by state and the nature of the crime.
  • For emergencies, please dial 100 or 112.
